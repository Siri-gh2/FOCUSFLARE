About FocusFlare 🔥

What is FocusFlare?
A productivity timer that helps you stay focused and take intentional breaks with a sprinkle of vibes and cuteness.

What is Pomodoro?
A Pomodoro is one focus session (traditionally 25 mins) followed by a short break (5 mins). After 4, take a long break. BUT — with FocusFlare, YOU choose the times!

How to Use:
1. Set your focus and break durations.
2. Click Start to begin.
3. Pomodoro count increases when focus finishes.
4. Enjoy breaks. Stay fresh. Stay slaying! 💖
